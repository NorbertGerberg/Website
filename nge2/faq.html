FAQ

Here are the answers to some of the most frequently asked questions regarding NGE and its license.


Q: For whom is this?

A: This engine was made for easy software and game development for PC platforms... so programmers.


Q: Can I make X with it?

A: This is more of a framework than a complete engine. So yes, you can make X with it.


Q: Is Credit necessary?

A: No, although I would appreciate a small mention in the credits or the engine's logo displayed somewhere visible.
     Please note that the license agreement must be included with every copy of your product that uses NGE, even if it only uses some code.


Q: Can I sell software made with NGE?

A: Yes, you can modify the engine and sell software powered by NGE completely free of charge. No royalties attached.
